Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium services.

Are you insured and bonded in Georgia?
Yes, absolutely. We carry full liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage to protect both your business and our staff across Metro Atlanta. We can provide a certificate of insurance (COI) upon request.
How quickly can you start service?
We move fast. We can typically perform a site walkthrough within 24 hours of your call and begin service as soon as the agreement is signed. For emergency cleanings, we often have same-day availability.
Do I need to sign a long-term contract?
We offer flexible agreements. While annual plans lock in the best rates, we also provide month-to-month options. We believe our consistent quality should be the reason you stay, not a binding contract.
Do you offer eco-friendly "Green Cleaning"?
Yes! We offer a "Green Clean" program upon request, utilizing EPA-certified, non-toxic products and HEPA filtration vacuums. This is ideal for LEED-certified buildings and offices with sensitive staff.
Can you clean during business hours?
Absolutely. Our "Day Porters" are trained to work discreetly around your staff without disrupting productivity. We also offer after-hours and weekend shifts to suit your operational needs.
Do you provide the cleaning supplies?
Yes, we bring our own high-quality, commercial-grade equipment and cleaning solutions. We can also manage your inventory of consumables (toilet paper, hand soap, trash liners) so you never run out.
What happens if I'm not satisfied?
We stand by our 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. If you spot any issue, simply notify us within 24 hours, and we will return to correct it at no extra charge. Your peace of mind is our priority.
